Missing Sound Effects for Submarine Commander (SUBCOM13)
=======================================================

Remaining sounds not yet added to sound/machines/submarine/.


SUBMARINE OPERATIONS
--------------------

sound/machines/submarine/hull_breach.ogg
  Description: Catastrophic hull breach. Loud tearing metal followed by
  rushing water. Dramatic, ~3-5s.
  Used by: breach_hull proc. (Currently uses flood.ogg + crash.ogg)

sound/machines/submarine/flooding_start.ogg
  Description: Water beginning to pour into a compartment. Gurgling
  rush of water through a crack. ~2-3s, loopable.
  Used by: add_water() when water_depth starts increasing.


PROPULSION & MACHINERY
----------------------

sound/machines/submarine/engine_nuclear_hum.ogg
  Description: Nuclear reactor ambient. Low, steady electromagnetic hum.
  Subtle, almost subliminal. Seamless loop, ~10s.
  Used by: reactor panel background when reactor is online.


ENVIRONMENT & UI
----------------

sound/machines/submarine/valve_turn.ogg
  Description: Heavy valve wheel being turned. Metal-on-metal
  grinding. ~1-2s.
  Used by: ballast valve, manual valve operations.

sound/machines/submarine/scrubber_hum.ogg
  Description: CO2 scrubber running. Steady fan/motor hum with
  slight air-rushing sound. Seamless loop, ~6s.
  Used by: scrubber active ambient.

sound/machines/submarine/radio_static.ogg
  Description: Radio transmission static. Brief burst of white noise
  before message. ~1s. Can be mixed with voice effect.
  Used by: radio console message reception.

sound/machines/submarine/water_drip.ogg
  Description: Water dripping in a flooded compartment. Isolated
  drip with echo. ~1s, randomizable.
  Used by: ambient sound in compartments with water_depth > 0.
