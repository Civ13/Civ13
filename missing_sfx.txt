Missing Sound Effects for Submarine Commander (SUBCOM13)
========================================================

Each entry lists the file path where the sound should be placed,
the suggested filename, and a description of what it should sound like.

SUBMARINE OPERATIONS
--------------------

sound/submarine/sonar_ping.ogg
  Description: Active sonar ping. A sharp, echoing "ping" with metallic reverb.
  Should sound like a real sonar: a brief 1-2kHz tone that decays with
  a 1-2 second echo tail. Mono, short duration (~3s).
  Used by: sonar panel active ping.

sound/submarine/sonar_passive.ogg
  Description: Passive sonar ambient loop. Low drone of ocean noise,
  distant machinery hum, occasional creak. Seamless loop, ~10s.
  Used by: sonar panel passive mode background.

sound/submarine/hull_creak.ogg
  Description: Metal hull creaking under pressure at depth. A low groaning
  sound of stressed metal plates. Should be unsettling. ~2-4s.
  Used by: depth pressure warnings, hull damage events.

sound/submarine/hull_breach.ogg
  Description: Catastrophic hull breach. Loud tearing metal followed by
  rushing water. Dramatic, ~3-5s.
  Used by: when a hull section loses integrity (breach_hull proc).

sound/submarine/flooding_start.ogg
  Description: Water beginning to pour into a compartment. Gurgling
  rush of water through a crack. ~2-3s, loopable.
  Used by: add_water() when water_depth starts increasing.

sound/submarine/flooding_ambient.ogg
  Description: Standing water in a compartment. Gentle sloshing and
  dripping. Seamless loop, ~8s.
  Used by: ambient sound when water_depth > 0 on a deck tile.

sound/submarine/alarm_dive.ogg
  Description: Submarine dive alarm. Classic "AWOOGA" klaxon.
  Two-tone rising and falling. ~3s, loopable.
  Used by: diving below periscope depth.

sound/submarine/alarm_surface.ogg
  Description: Surface alarm. Distinct from dive alarm. Higher pitched,
  urgent beeping pattern. ~3s, loopable.
  Used by: surfacing operations.

sound/submarine/alarm_flood.ogg
  Description: Flooding alarm. Loud repeating buzzer. ~2s, loopable.
  Used by: flooding controller when a compartment starts flooding.

sound/submarine/alarm_depth.ogg
  Description: Critical depth warning. Rapid high-pitched alarm.
  ~2s, loopable. Should feel urgent and dangerous.
  Used by: approaching crush depth.

sound/submarine/alarm_rig.ogg
  Description: General quarters / battle stations alarm. Deep,
  resonant klaxon. ~3s, loopable.
  Used by: combat encounters, mission start.

PROPULSION & MACHINERY
----------------------

sound/submarine/engine_nuclear_hum.ogg
  Description: Nuclear reactor ambient. Low, steady electromagnetic hum.
  Subtle, almost subliminal. Seamless loop, ~10s.
  Used by: reactor panel background when reactor is online.

sound/submarine/engine_diesel_run.ogg
  Description: Diesel generator running. Rhythmic chugging of a large
  marine diesel. Steady beat. Seamless loop, ~8s.
  Used by: diesel engine when throttle > 0.

sound/submarine/engine_diesel_start.ogg
  Description: Diesel generator cranking and starting. Starter motor
  whine followed by diesel catching and settling to idle. ~4-5s.
  Used by: first engagement of diesel throttle.

sound/submarine/turbine_run.ogg
  Description: Steam turbine spinning. High-pitched whine with
  underlying rumble. Seamless loop, ~8s.
  Used by: steam turbine at speed.

sound/submarine/propeller_water.ogg
  Description: Propeller churning water. Bubbling, rushing water
  sound from screw rotation. Seamless loop, ~6s.
  Used by: ambient sound when sub is moving.

WEAPONS
-------

sound/submarine/torpedo_launch.ogg
  Description: Torpedo firing from tube. Compressed air release hiss
  followed by a deep "thump" and rushing water. ~2-3s.
  Used by: launch_torpedo() proc.

sound/submarine/torpedo_travel.ogg
  Description: Torpedo running through water. Propeller whine with
  cavitation bubbles. Seamless loop, ~5s.
  Used by: torpedo_datum while torpedo is in transit.

sound/submarine/torpedo_explosion.ogg
  Description: Underwater detonation. Deep, muffled boom with
  shockwave rumble. Not a sharp crack - underwater explosions
  are bass-heavy. ~3-4s.
  Used by: torpedo detonation.

sound/submarine/depth_charge_distant.ogg
  Description: Distant depth charge. Muffled thud, felt more than
  heard. Low frequency rumble. ~2-3s.
  Used by: depth charge detonation at range.

sound/submarine/depth_charge_close.ogg
  Description: Close depth charge detonation. Violent underwater
  explosion with shockwave. Loud, bass-heavy. ~3-5s.
  Used by: depth charge detonation nearby.

sound/submarine/gunfire_surface.ogg
  Description: Naval gunfire from surface vessel. Distant booming
  reports. ~1-2s per shot.
  Used by: NPC gun attacks.

sound/submarine/missile_launch.ogg
  Description: Missile launch from above. Rising whoosh with
  crackling exhaust. ~2-3s.
  Used by: NPC missile attacks (aircraft).

ENVIRONMENT & UI
----------------

sound/submarine/console_click.ogg
  Description: Physical button press on a submarine console.
  Mechanical click with slight metallic ring. ~0.5s.
  Used by: all console button presses. (Can reuse existing
  sound/machines/click.ogg if suitable.)

sound/submarine/console_switch.ogg
  Description: Toggle switch flip. Solid mechanical snap. ~0.3s.
  Used by: vent toggles, ballast valve, bilge pump switches.

sound/submarine/valve_turn.ogg
  Description: Heavy valve wheel being turned. Metal-on-metal
  grinding. ~1-2s.
  Used by: ballast valve, manual valve operations.

sound/submarine/scrubber_hum.ogg
  Description: CO2 scrubber running. Steady fan/motor hum with
  slight air-rushing sound. Seamless loop, ~6s.
  Used by: scrubber active ambient.

sound/submarine/bilge_pump_run.ogg
  Description: Bilge pump operating. Rhythmic pumping sound with
  water gurgling. Seamless loop, ~6s.
  Used by: bilge pump active ambient.

sound/submarine/radio_static.ogg
  Description: Radio transmission static. Brief burst of white noise
  before message. ~1s. Can be mixed with voice effect.
  Used by: radio console message reception.

sound/submarine/water_drip.ogg
  Description: Water dripping in a flooded compartment. Isolated
  drip with echo. ~1s, randomizable.
  Used by: ambient sound in compartments with water_depth > 0.

sound/submarine/radiation_alarm.ogg
  Description: Radiation warning alarm. Geiger counter clicking
  accelerating into a whine. ~3s, loopable.
  Used by: reactor_core radiation_pulse proc.
